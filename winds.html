Instrument Information
Flute The flute creates a high ptched tone suitable for melodies. It isn't a member of the reed family, and Student flutes are nickel silver, while professional flutes are silver or plated with silver.
Piccolo The piccolo is the highest pitched woodwind. It is made of wood and plays an octave higher than the flute.
Oboe The oboe is a double reed woodwind; its reeds are made from two cane peices that are attached together. They play on high registers.
Clarinet The clarinet is a reed instrument. It is played by attaching the reed to the mouthpiece with a wire piece called the ligature, which is a very versatile instrument, used in classical and jazz music.
Bassoon The bassoon is a double reed instrument; its reeds are made from two cane. It plays on lower registers.
