Violin
The smallest of the bowed strings, the violin hits the highest registers of all stringed instruments. Its four strings are G3, D4, A4, and E5.

Viola
Just a little bigger than the violin, the viola has a lower and deeper sound than the violin but a very similar timbre to the violin. Its four strings are C3, G3, D4, and A4.

Cello
The cello is much bigger than the violin abnd the viola with a much deeper sound, hitting lower sound registers. Its four strings are C2, G2, D3, and A3, just like the viola, but one total octave lower!

Contrabass (Double Bass)
The largest of the bowed strings, the contrabass reaches the lowers registers and has the deepest sound. Its four strings are E1, A1, D2, G2

Harp
The Harp is a plucked string instrument that inspired the piano. It ranges from C♭1 to G♭7.

Guitar
Very popular in 21st century music, the Guitar is a plucked 6-string instrument that provides great harmonies and melodic vocal accompaniment. Its 6 strings are E2, A2, D3, G3, G3, B4, and E4.
