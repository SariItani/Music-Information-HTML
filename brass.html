Instrument Information about the Instrument
Trumpet It is played by blowing into it while pressing either of its three valves, and it also uses slides
French Horn Unlike the Trombone and the Trumpet, the French horn has rotatory valves instead of piston valves.
Trombone It is played by blowing into it by the use of a hand slide, which has positions that produce different notes.
Tuba It is the largest of all brass instruments. It has the lowest registers and thus acts as the base in bands and Orchestra.
